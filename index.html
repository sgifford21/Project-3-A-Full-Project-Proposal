Welcome to The Locker Room

The Locker Room is a website prototype for a former apparel business I operated while stationed at Clay Kaserne in Germany. The business focused on customizable clothing and spirit wear for soldiers, military families, teams, and community members.

This site highlights the concept of an online storefront while staying within the project scope: a simple, professional two-page website built with HTML and CSS.

Featured Products

Custom T-Shirts

Personalized shirts for family events, team activities, unit pride, and everyday wear.

Hoodies & Sweatshirts

Comfortable apparel options designed for school spirit, military community events, and gifts.

Team & Spirit Wear

Coordinated apparel concepts for sports teams, youth groups, and community organizations.

Custom Orders

This prototype does not include real ordering or payment features. Instead, it demonstrates how a future storefront could present product options, explain customization services, and provide contact information for customer questions.

Contact Concept

For a real version of this website, customers would be able to request information about designs, sizing, colors, and order timelines. This project focuses on the layout and content structure only.
